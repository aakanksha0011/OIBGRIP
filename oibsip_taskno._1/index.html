Famous Place

Daulatabad Fort

Daulatabad Qilla Aaa Fort is a frozen time capsule. Its location is on the top of a cone-shaped mountain in Aurangabad, about 200 meters above sea level. Its strategic location, incredible architecture and three-tiered defence system are wonderful. They make it one of the most powerful mountain forts in the state.Also known as Devgiri Fort.

Bibi Ka Maqbara

Situated about 3 km from the city is Bibi Ka Maqbara, the burial place of Aurangzeb’s wife, Rabia-ud-Durrani. It is an imitation of the Taj at Agra, and, due to its similar design, it is popularly known as the Mini Taj of the Deccan.Built on an elevated platform, it is guarded by four minarets along its four corners and can be reached by a series of steps from three different sides.
